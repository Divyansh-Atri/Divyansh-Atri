### `~/projects --featured`

```text
┌─ numerical & stochastic ──────────────────────────────────────────────┐
```

**[`metastability-exit-times`](https://github.com/Divyansh-Atri/metastability-exit-times)** — `Jupyter`
Numerical study of metastability and noise-induced rare transitions in stochastic
differential equations, focused on overdamped Langevin dynamics.

**[`stochastic-control-hjb`](https://github.com/Divyansh-Atri/stochastic-control-hjb)** — `Jupyter`
Stochastic control problems solved via Hamilton–Jacobi–Bellman equations and
dynamic programming.

**[`Numerical-SDE-Methods`](https://github.com/Divyansh-Atri/Numerical-SDE-Methods)** — `Python`
From-scratch implementations of major numerical SDE methods, with controlled
experiments validating their theoretical convergence — no black-box solvers.

**[`Stochastic-Optimization-Landscape`](https://github.com/Divyansh-Atri/Stochastic-Optimization-Landscape)** — `Python`
Gradient Descent, SGD, and Adam built from scratch, with mathematical analysis
and experiments on convex and non-convex landscapes.

**[`Random-Matrix-Spectra`](https://github.com/Divyansh-Atri/Random-Matrix-Spectra)** — `Jupyter`
Empirical investigation of eigenvalue distributions of large random matrices.

**[`Numerical-Stability-Lab`](https://github.com/Divyansh-Atri/Numerical-Stability-Lab)** — `Jupyter`
Study of numerical stability, conditioning, and floating-point error propagation
in core algorithms.

```text
┌─ quantum & systems ───────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
```

**[`Quantum-Algorithm`](https://github.com/Divyansh-Atri/Quantum--Algorithm)** — `Python · Qiskit`
Grover's, Shor's, Deutsch–Jozsa, Quantum Random Walk, and QKD, with circuit
diagrams and performance analysis.

**[`XV-6`](https://github.com/Divyansh-Atri/XV-6)** — `C · RISC-V`
Preemptive priority-based scheduling in xv6, with dynamic priority computation,
custom system calls, and multi-CPU performance evaluation.

**[`C-Shell`](https://github.com/Divyansh-Atri/C-Shell)** — `C`
Unix shell with I/O redirection, piping, process management, signal handling,
and job control.
